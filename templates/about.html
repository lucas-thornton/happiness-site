Country Breakdown




To reduce the impact of extreme diffences in values between countries when modelling happiness we identified an important first step to be breaking the countries down into groups so that they're only being compared with countries of similar economic and social levels.

In order to create this breakdown we thought it would be best to so a scatter plot of GDP per capita vs infant mortality rate (per 1000 born). In our mind this would illustrate countries that are both economically strong whilst socially concious.

Using a Kmeans test broke down the scatter plot into 3 groups, which we labeleld as Developed (yellow), Developing (purple) and Underdeveloped (aqua).
